Maccor and Webasto Announce Strategic Partnership to Expand Battery Testing Capabilities

Tulsa, OK – Maccor, a leading provider of battery test systems, is excited to announce a strategic collaboration with Webasto to enhance testing solutions across a wider range of voltages. Under this partnership, Maccor will focus on providing high-precision testing for lower-voltage systems (<200V), while Webasto will specialize in higher-voltage systems (>200V).

Both Maccor and Webasto proudly manufacture their systems in the United States, ensuring the highest standards of quality, reliability, and support for their customers. This collaboration leverages the strengths of both American-made companies to deliver comprehensive battery testing capabilities, supporting research, development, and manufacturing across industries.

“We are thrilled to work with Webasto,” said Mike Sandoval, Vice President of Business Development Maccor. “This partnership allows us to provide our customers with best-in-class solutions across the full voltage spectrum. We look forward to a fruitful relationship that drives innovation and supports the growing demands of the battery industry.”
